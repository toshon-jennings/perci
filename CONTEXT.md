# Context

## Glossary

### Perci Map District

A named neighborhood on the Perci Map that groups surfaces by their real
workspace role. Districts are not visual decoration; they define the planner's
mental model for where surfaces belong.

Current districts are Core Concourse, Knowledge Quarter, Creation Yard,
Operations Terminal, Local Systems Depot, and Business Office.

### Perci Map Route

A typed relationship line between surfaces and districts. A route explains a
kind of activity crossing the workspace, such as movement, shared context,
agent work, build output, research, local runtime, governance, or expenses.

### Perci Map Station

A launchable Perci surface shown on the map. Each station belongs to exactly
one district and may sit on multiple routes.
